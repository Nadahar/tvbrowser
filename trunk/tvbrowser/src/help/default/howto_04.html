Installing plug-ins

Plug-ins are distributed as .jar files.

After having downloaded a plug-in from the internet you'll need to copy it to the plugins subfolder of the TV-Browser folder.

Open the settings dialog by pressing Ctrl-S or by choosing Settings from the TV-Browser menu. Activate the Plugins tab.

Move the plug-in from the left to the right area. The plug-in is now installed and ready for use.

Hint: For informations concerning new plug-ins visit our home page:
http://tvbrowser.sourceforge.net
